Tiramisu Recipe

Here is a simple recipe for a delicious Tiramisu



Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pound mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 3 tablespoons rum
  • 24 ladyfinger cookies
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

Instructions

First Step

In a medium saucepan, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until well blended.

Second Step

Whisk in milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils.

Third Step

Remove from heat and allow to cool slightly. Cover tightly and chill in refrigerator 1 hour.

Fourth Step

In a medium bowl, beat cream with vanilla until stiff peaks form.

Fifth Step

Whisk mascarpone into yolk mixture until smooth.

Sixth Step

Combine coffee and rum. Split ladyfingers in half lengthwise and drizzle with coffee mixture.

Seventh Step

Arrange half of soaked ladyfingers. Spread half of mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers, then half of whipped cream over that.

Eighth Step

Sprinkle cocoa powder on top of tiramisu

Final Step

Refrigerate for 4h and youre ready to serve
